About me

UX + Motion Designer

Hi, I am Cindy. I am a UX/Motion designer who currently studies at Carnegie Mellon University. I have a major in design and a minor in Fine Arts. I am well experienced in the UX design of apps and websites. I went to CMU because I love design and its potential impact on the tech industry. I worked as a design intern for Ogilvy, where I gained visual and motion design skills. I worked at Root insurance, where I applied my design thinking skill to solve business problems. Besides, I worked as a commissioned animator for dyslexiefont.com to create their promotional video to improve the learning experience for people with reading disabilities. From making the video, I become proficient in storytelling with motion. Feel free to come and say hi at anytime!
